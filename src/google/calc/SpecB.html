Basic Sums

As a business customer I want to automate my basic sums so that I can get correct answers quickly.

Acceptance Criteria

  1. I can add, subtract, multiply or divide any two numbers.

Example

Calculating the following sums shows the expected result.

Sum Expected Result
9 * 7 9 * 7 = 63
39 / 3 39 / 3 = 13
8 - 14 8 - 14 = -6
1 / 0 1 / 0 = NaN
